Introduction

What is land pollution?

The major types of pollution

Air, water, and land pollution.


There are three main types of pollution: air pollution, water pollution, and land pollution. This website is mainly about land pollution, but there will be basic information about the other two types of pollution below.


Air pollution

Air pollution is caused by the release of greenhouse gases like carbon dioxide into the air. It can also be caused by volcanic eruptions. However, we release a lot more greenhouse gases in the air than volcanoes, mostly by burning greenhouse gases in factories.

This type of pollution can be very harmful for us, increasing the risk of heart diseases, lung cancer and some types of respiratory infections. It also creates an effect called the greenhouse effect, which makes the earth hotter. The result of a rise in temperature will lead to many problems.


Water pollution

Water pollution is the contamination of water. If polluted water is consumed, it can cause many health problems because it contains waste like plastic, bacteria, and other microorganisms. When fish consume plastic, they can get sick and die. However, if we eat the fish, we can also eat the plastic if we are not careful enough.

There are many causes of water pollution. First, oil and natural gas can enter rivers and lakes from underground locations, polluting the water. We can also contribute to water pollution by dumping waste into the ocean. Sometimes oil can spill from boats in an accident into the water.


Introduction to land pollution


Land pollution is the contamination of soil with dangerous chemicals. Sometimes, plants can absorb fertilizers in the air. If these plants are eaten, the dangerous chemicals in fertilizers can harm our body in many different ways.


The story of DDT

DDT (also called dicholorodiphenyltrichloroethane) is a powerful pesticide that kills pests that destroy plants and spread diseases. However, this pesticide can be a big threat to the environment.

When DDT is sprayed on the plants, some of it can get washed into rivers by the rain. Sometimes, the animals that live there eat the DDT. However, the amount of DDT swallowed is so small that these animals won't be harmed. The problem with DDT is that it does not break down inside the animal's bodies, but is stored inside them.

Soon, other animals like small fish eat the animals containing DDT, and bigger fish eat these small fish. Fortunately, the amount of DDT consumed is not enough to harm them. Herons eat lots of these big fish. Now the amoutn of DDT inside the heron can affect the strength of the eggshells, so the population of herons in the world decreases.

The good news is that now DDT is banned on mnay countries, but some countries still use it as it is the best way to slow down the spread of malaria, a disease carried by mosquitoes.

Trash on a beach in Hawaii

Introduction

Litter is made of waste like plastic that we make that is not disposed at the correct location. For example, people who litter can throw trash on the street, forest, or ocean.


What happens after we litter?

The waste that we litter sometimes gets washed into the ocean. The waste in oceans tend to wash to 5 different locations, called garbage patches. However, out of these 5 garbage patches, the largest garbage patch is the Great pacific Gargabe Patch, which consists of over 1.8 trillion plastic pieces. Conbined, they weigh around 80 million kilograms. This patch could be very dangerous to the animals living near it.

An oil platform over the ocean, used for getting petroleum and natural gas

Introduction

We mine to get many different types of minerals from deep in the soil, like metals, coal, gemstones, chalk, and limestone. There are a few methods of mining. The first method, underground mining is very expensive to make but get better results. The second method, surface mining is cheaper but get worse results. The third method, placer mining separates minerals in the form of sediments from water in rivers, beaches, and more. The last method, in-situ mining is done by dissolving the mineral, then bringing it to the surface.


Why is mining bad for the environment?

Sometimes some accidents can happen, and various harmful or even toxic chemicals can wash away to rivers and contaminate it. This is very bad for the animals living there.

Introduction

Land pollution can be caused by agriculture in many ways. One example is the use of artificial fertilizers and pesticides. These pesticides contain chemicals that help the plant grow, but they also contain chemicals that are harmful or toxic to animals. These fertilizers can reach the ocean or river and harm the animals in it.


How can fertilizers reach the ocean or river?

Rain falls down from the sky into farms. These pesticides go inside the water and flow along with it. Over time, the water reaches the ocean, carrying the harmful fertilizer. Sometimes, these fertilizers can dissolve in the water, making it really hard to see. The fertilizers in the ocean can also make it unhealthy to drink.

Introduction

When people litter, sometimes the waste can wash away to rivers and oceans. Now, this plastic waste is very dangerous to animals when swallowed. Over time, these plastics break into smaller pieces of plastics called microplastics. These microplastics are very small, usually less than 5 millimeters in length. This makes it easier for the animals to accidentaly eat or swallow the plastics when they are trying to eat other food and could die. Because of this, there could be a decline in marine population, decreasing the food supply in the world, which will be very dangerous for people.


Why can this be dangerous for us?

First, more marine creatures will die, leading to a food shortage crisis. Even though we get the fish, they still have microplastics in them, which are really hard to identify. These fish could end up in our stomach, and if we are not careful enough, we could also accidentally swallow these microplastics in the fishes. This could lead to many health problems.


arrow_backward Sick animals



A picture of krill, a food source for many animals living in the ocean


Little pieces of microplastics collected from rivers in Germany


A picture of an albatross with lots of pieces of trash inside it


Introduction

The plastic in the ocean can harm marine animals like fish and turtles. Sometimes the animals can swallow the plastic, or they can get stuck in it. There are over 250 million killograms of trash in the ocean, so it would be really easy for marine animals to accidentally eat these plastics, and the plastic won't ever run out, since people are still throwing waste into the ocean.


Why do marine creatures eat plastic?

There are 2 main reasons for this. First, some big pieces of plastic, like plastic bags can look like food. For example, a big plastic bag can look like a jellyfish. Turtles, who each jellyfish, can end up eating these plastic bags and get sick. The other reason is that waste that we produce can smell like krill over time. When krill consumes algae, it emits a chemical called dimethyl sulfide or DMS. However, when we throw away plastic, the plastic creates a perfect place for the algae to live in. As a result, the algae emits the dimethyl sulfide or DMS from the plastic bag and animals can mistakenly eat the plastic bag, thinking it is just krill.

Introduction

Some chemicals can pollute soil or water to make them unsuitable for plants and animals to live in. Sometimes, acid rain, ammonium in fertilizers, oxidation, and other processes can make the soil more acidic or basic. This can have some effects on plant growth. If the pH level is too high or too low, then there could be high concentrations of chemicals like lead, which can be toxic to plants.


How to fix the acidity of soil?

When the pH of soil is too acidic or too basic, farmers use a process called neutralization. This process is done by combining an acid with a base. They react to form neutral components. For example, when you mix hydrochloric acid (a strong acid) with sodium hydroxide (a strong base), they neutralize each other to form sodium chloride (common salt) and water. Farmers usually use limestone to neutralize acidic soil since it is slightly basic with a pH of around 7.5-8, while sometimes they can use sphagnum peat, which has a pH of around 3-4.5, for basic soil.

arrow_backward The 3Rs



A cotton bag, a better alternative to plastic bags


Recycling bins are used to separate different types of trash


This picture predicts that there will be more trash than fish in the ocean by 2050, so we need to reduce, if not stop, the use of single-use plastic.


Introduction

The 3Rs stand for Reuse, Reduce, and Recycle. First, reuse means to use something for more than once. For example, instead of using plastic bags once, you can use them again. Next, reduce means to use less of something. Instead of using 4 plastic bags a day, try to reduce it to only 1-2 plastic bags a week. Last, recycle is similar to reuse, except that you use items again for a different purpose. People crush used newspapers to make other things like books, magazines, and even new newspapers.


What are the benefits?

First, this would be better for the environment, as we throw less waste, so we will contribute less to land pollution. Because of that, we can start cleaning up land pollution faster than we can throw trash. Second, it would be cheaper for us. Nowadays, countries are forcing people to pay for plastic bags, so hopefully people will use less plastic bags. Alternatively, instead of using plastic bags, we can buy cotton bags. These bags last a long time.

Introduction

Even though we do the 3Rs, it isn't enough to make any big change in the world. As a result, people have made organizations for a lot of people to work together to clean many places like rivers, streets, and forests. Although one person can't do much to stop land pollution, many people working together could have a big effect on land pollution. One of these organizations is World Cleanup Day.


World Cleanup Day

World Cleanup Day is a worldwide organization that is held for a day every year (specifically the third Saturday of each September). The event for 2022 will be held on 17 September. In 2021, World Cleanup Day was held on 18 September with 8.5 million people from 191 countries collecting over 33 thousand tons of waste.

Team Trees

Team Trees is a fundraising campaign by MrBeast and Mark Rober, and aimed to plant 20 million trees. It worked by donating a certain amount of money (like 10 dollars) and then planting the amount of money that you give (in the previous case, 10 trees will be planted). It was a success, and by the end of 2020, over 23 million dollars were donated. However, some people said that it wouldn't do much as we are still emitting greenhouse gases at a very fast rate, but Team Trees spread awareness about land pollution to millions of people.
